• Click 'Select Lift' to choose your movement
  • Click 'Add Weight' to select reps and weight
  • Use the clock button to see your history
  • Plus and minus buttons add/remove sets and lifts
  • Check button completes the workout
  • Press and hold a lift card for a super-set divider
  • Combine the filters up top to search for your workouts
  • Click the garbage icon to delete a workout
  • Click the arrow icon to load the workout to the editor
  • Press the refresh button to clear all filters
  • Only days which you have lifted will show as options
  • For a given lift and number of reps, the weekly max of each daily max is shown
  • Click the table button to see all daily max's listed
  • Set your goals underneath the chart area
  • Change the timeframe with the buttons below
  • You need to lift for a few weeks to get meaningful data we can chart!
  • Use the presets or the sliders to set your timer
